                README (how to run)

                Open index.html in any modern browser. Everything runs locally, and data persists in localStorage. Use Export/Import in Settings to back up or move data. Click "Print Invoice" in the header or an invoice row to open the printable view.

                Onboarding Tips

                Quick tour

                • Use sidebar or Ctrl/Cmd+1-4 to jump views; Ctrl/Cmd+S saves current form.
                • Dashboard chips: add client, log arrival, templates, JoshHub, calendar feed.
                • Reconcile tab: filters, bulk mark paid, finance summary, CSV exports.
                • Appointments: launch ParrisTechApp; summary auto-ingest on return.
                • Settings: brand colors/logo, seeds, encrypted export, dry-run import.
